Astro Bot

Astro Bot Rescue Mission is a highly praised VR platformer for PlayStation VR. Players control Astro, a small robot, on a mission to rescue his crew. The game features innovative VR mechanics, creative level design, and fun puzzles, offering an immersive and unique experience.

Metaphor: ReFantazio

Metaphor: ReFantazio is a turn-based RPG from the creators of Persona and Shin Megami Tensei. Set in a fantasy world where myth and reality blur, the game features classic RPG elements, a unique art style, and a captivating story.

Final Fantasy VII Rebirth

Final Fantasy VII Rebirth is the second part of the Final Fantasy VII Remake, continuing Cloud Strife's journey. Set in an expansive open world, it expands the original story with new gameplay mechanics, deeper character development, and a focus on the battle against Shinra and Sephiroth.
